Our Scorecard Partnership

Catastrophic Wildfire Prevention & Consortium (CWP&C) at CrowdDoing

CWP&C is working to establish the collective opportunity for net risk reduction by communities, achieved through multi-stakeholder collaboration using a costs and liabilities of inaction model to finance action to prevent wildfire risks, and with a synergistic portfolio of new and traditional strategies for prevention, mitigation and adaptation.

CrowdDoing.World (CD) is a joint initiative of the Match4Action Foundation and ReframeIt, focused on addressing the social, economic, and environmental challenges our world faces by collaborating with professionals and volunteers from many different industries. CD aims to support social innovations with transformative impact potential through global multi-disciplinary volunteering, micro-leadership and service learning.

ARISE - US

ARISE-US is the US network of ARISE2 , established by the United Nations (UN) to build public-private collaboration in disaster resilience. We are a 100% volunteer organization operating in 35 countries worldwide, with some 450 corporate members and many more NGO and governmental partners. We published the original UN City Disaster Resilience Scorecard, now used by roughly 350 cities world-wide (including many in the US), on which this wildfire-specific scorecard is modeled, as well as several other scorecards, and a free critical asset management tool.
